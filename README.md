# 📊 HR Analytics Dashboard – Tableau Project

## 🔍 Project Overview
This project presents an interactive **HR Analytics Dashboard** built using **Tableau** to analyze workforce data and identify employee attrition patterns. The dashboard provides HR professionals and business leaders with actionable insights into employee demographics, attrition trends, job satisfaction levels, and workforce distribution, enabling data-driven decision-making for employee retention and workforce planning.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Monitor overall employee and attrition metrics.
- Identify departments with the highest employee turnover.
- Analyze attrition across age groups, gender, and education fields.
- Evaluate employee job satisfaction across different job roles.
- Support HR teams in developing effective retention strategies.

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies
- Tableau Public
- Microsoft Excel
- Data Visualization
- HR Analytics

---

## 📈 Dashboard Highlights

### Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
- **Total Employees:** 1,470
- **Attrition Count:** 237
- **Attrition Rate:** 16.12%
- **Active Employees:** 1,233
- **Average Employee Age:** 37

### Visualizations Included
- Department-wise Attrition Analysis
- Employee Distribution by Age Group
- Job Satisfaction Rating by Job Role
- Education Field-wise Attrition
- Gender-based Attrition Analysis
- Attrition Rate by Gender Across Different Age Groups
- Interactive Education Filter

---

## 💡 Key Insights
- The **Sales Department** recorded the highest employee attrition, followed by **R&D**.
- Employees between the **30–36 age group** represent the largest segment of the workforce.
- **Life Sciences** and **Medical** education backgrounds show the highest attrition counts.
- Job satisfaction levels vary significantly across different job roles.
- Gender-based attrition patterns differ across age groups, helping identify high-risk employee segments.

---

## 📌 Business Value
This dashboard helps HR professionals:
- Monitor workforce health through key HR metrics.
- Identify departments and employee groups with high attrition risk.
- Understand employee demographics and satisfaction trends.
- Develop data-driven employee retention strategies.
